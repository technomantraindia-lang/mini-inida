OUR STORY & VISION

A Landmark of Hospitality & Culinary Art

Mini-India Food & Beverage Co., Ltd is one of Thailand's premier Indian hospitality and entertainment destinations, offering authentic Indian cuisine, luxury dining, pool parties, and world-class event facilities. With a seating capacity of over 1,000 guests, we provide unforgettable experiences through exceptional food, entertainment, and cultural celebrations.

Established in 2022 and located at the prestigious Mini-Siam Campus in Pattaya, our multi-concept landmark houses 3 distinct premium bars, authentic vegetarian and non-vegetarian banquet halls, and outdoor poolside facilities.

Our mission is to bring the rich heritage of Indian cuisine and wedding celebrations together with Thailand's vibrant entertainment and hospitality standards. Whether you are seeking a royal vegetarian thali, fresh premium seafood, a high-energy DJ pool party, or a wedding venue for 1,000 guests, Mini-India delivers with unmatched excellence.

THE MINI-INDIA DIFFERENCE

The Three Pillars of Our Landmark

I

THE CULINARY PALACE

Authentic Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian cuisines, alongside curated Korean, Japanese (including top sushi), Chinese, and Thai menus. Handcrafted by native specialist chefs using imported native spices and fresh coastal ingredients.

II

THE OASIS DECK & BARS

Featuring 3 distinct premium bars, custom cabanas, an energetic swimming pool zone, Bollywood theme nights, and live fire dancing. A high-energy experience matching Pattaya's legendary entertainment vibes.

III

GRAND EVENT SPACES

Fulfilling landmark destination weddings, conferences, and corporate celebrations. Boasting a massive 1,000 guest seating capacity Gala venue, alongside an exclusive 200-capacity VIP lounge dedicated to full premium events.
