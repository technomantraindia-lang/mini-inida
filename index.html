WHO WE ARE

Pattaya's Premier Hospitality & Entertainment Landmark

Mini-India Food & Beverage Co., Ltd is not simply a restaurant—we are Pattaya's grandest multi-concept dining and entertainment destination. Spanning across our grand campus, we feature 3 distinct premium bars and an extraordinary multi-cuisine menu offering authentic Vegetarian, Non-Vegetarian, Korean, Japanese (featuring top-grade sushi), Chinese, and Thai delicacies.

For grand celebrations, we boast a magnificent Gala and Party Venue with a seating capacity of 1,000 guests, alongside an exclusive, ultra-luxury 200-capacity premium lounge dedicated to the most prestigious VIP gatherings and premium celebrations.
